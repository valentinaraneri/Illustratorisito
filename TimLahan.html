Tim Lahan

Tim Lahan’s naughty, witty artwork is the product of his ability to digest and illustrate clever glitches.

“I generally aim to make things as simple as possible,” says Tim. “Sometimes they come out funny.”

The ADC Young Gun award winner’s instantly recognisable style is enviably simple and genuinely funny.

Generally commissioned to bring a touch of joy to magazines, brand campaigns and music.
