Karlotta Freier

The portfolio of LA-based illustrator Xoana Herrera is an explosion of sumptuous, stylish character scenes.

Xoana trained as a graphic designer in Argentina but over time found herself drawn to the world of illustration

She creates complex and accessible imagery which would be as at home in an ad campaign as in a children’s book

“As an artist I think it is up to us to keep evolving and taking creative decisions,” she says.
